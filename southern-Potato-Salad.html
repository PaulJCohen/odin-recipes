Southern Potato Salad

Description

A classic Southern Potato Salad, just like Grandma used to make it

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ stalk celery, chopped
  • ¼ cup sweet relish
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • salt and pepper to taste
